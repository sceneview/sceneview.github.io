Support SceneView

SceneView is a free, open source project built by passionate developers. Your support keeps it running, ad-free, and constantly improving.

Why Contribute

Why your support matters

🚫
Zero Advertisements
SceneView will never contain ads or track users. Sponsor contributions are what make this possible and keep the project completely free.
🔧
Better Hardware
Funds go directly toward AR-capable test devices, enabling more thorough testing across a broader range of hardware configurations.
⚡
Faster Development
Financial support frees core contributors to spend more time on features, bug fixes, documentation and community support.

Ways to Sponsor

🌐
Recommended for Companies
Open Collective
Contributions go into a communal, transparent fund used to support the entire SceneView developer community. Every transaction is publicly visible — full accountability, zero opacity. Gold, Diamond and Premiere sponsors get recognition in the repositories. If your business or content channel relies on SceneView as part of your income, this is the recommended way to give back.
